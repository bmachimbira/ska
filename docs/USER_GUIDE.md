## Getting Started

### Installation

1. **Download the App**
   - Open Google Play Store
   - Search for "SDA Content"
   - Tap "Install"

2. **First Launch**
   - Open the app
   - Grant necessary permissions (internet, notifications)
   - The home screen will load with featured content

### Home Screen

The home screen is your hub for all content:

- **Featured Sermons**: Carousel of specially selected sermons
- **Today's Devotional**: Quick access to the daily reading
- **Current Sabbath School**: Active quarterly lessons

Simply tap any item to explore!

## Features Overview

### Navigation

The app has a bottom navigation bar with four main sections:

1. **Home**: Featured content and quick access
2. **Sermons**: Browse video and audio messages
3. **Devotionals**: Daily spiritual readings
4. **Quarterlies**: Sabbath School lesson study

## Sermons

### Browsing Sermons

**To view all sermons:**
1. Tap "Sermons" in the bottom navigation
2. Scroll through the list
3. Use the search bar to find specific topics

**Filtering:**
- Search by title or speaker
- Filter by tag (e.g., "Prayer", "Faith", "Prophecy")
- Filter by series
- Filter by speaker

### Watching/Listening

**Video Playback:**
1. Tap any sermon to open details
2. Tap the video thumbnail
3. Video plays in full-screen mode
4. Supports:
   - Play/pause
   - Seek forward/backward
   - Quality selection
   - Picture-in-Picture (PiP) mode

**Audio Playback:**
1. On sermon detail screen, tap "Audio Only"
2. Plays in background
3. Control from notification
4. Lock screen controls available

**Features:**
- Adjustable playback speed
- Bookmark favorite sermons
- Share with friends
- Download for offline viewing

### Sharing Sermons

1. Open any sermon
2. Tap the share icon
3. Choose sharing method:
   - Social media
   - Email
   - Copy link
   - Direct message

## Daily Devotionals

### Reading Today's Devotional

**Quick Access:**
1. Tap "Today's Devotional" on home screen
2. OR tap "Devotionals" → "Today"

**What You'll See:**
- Date and title
- Author (usually Ellen G. White)
- Memory verse
- Daily reading
- Audio narration (tap play button)

### Archive

**Browse Past Devotionals:**
1. Go to Devotionals tab
2. Tap "Archive"
3. Scroll through calendar view
4. Tap any date to read that day's devotional

### Features

- **Audio Playback**: Listen while commuting
- **Adjustable Text Size**: Pinch to zoom or use settings
- **Share**: Send inspirational quotes to friends
- **Bookmark**: Save favorites for quick access

## Sabbath School Quarterlies

### Selecting a Quarterly

1. Go to Quarterlies tab
2. Choose from three categories:
   - **Adult**: Standard Bible study
   - **Youth**: Young adult focused
   - **Kids**: Children's lessons

3. Tap the current quarter to begin

### Studying Lessons

**Navigation:**
- Each quarterly has 13 lessons (one per week)
- Each lesson has 7 days (Sabbath through Friday)

**Reading a Lesson Day:**
1. Select your quarterly
2. Choose the current lesson
3. Tap the day you're studying
4. Read the content, including:
   - Memory verse
   - Daily study material
   - Questions for reflection

### Study Features

- **Memory Verse**: Highlighted at the top
- **Audio**: Listen to the lesson
- **Previous/Next**: Navigate between days easily
- **Progress Tracking**: See which lessons you've completed
- **Offline Access**: Download full quarterly for offline study

## AI Study Assistant

The AI Study Assistant helps you understand Bible topics, devotionals, and quarterly lessons with intelligent, context-aware responses.

### Accessing the Chat

1. Tap "Chat" icon or menu item
2. OR long-press any devotional/quarterly and select "Ask AI"

### Modes

**General Mode:**
- Ask any Bible or spiritual question
- Get answers from devotionals, quarterlies, and Bible

**Quarterly Mode:**
- Automatically activated from quarterly screen
- Questions scoped to current quarterly
- Helps with lesson understanding

**Devotional Mode:**
- Activated from devotional screen
- Context from current devotional

### Asking Questions

**Example Questions:**

*General:*
- "What does the Bible say about prayer?"
- "Explain the Sabbath in Scripture"
- "Who were the 12 disciples?"

*Quarterly:*
- "What is the main theme of this week's lesson?"
- "Explain the memory verse in context"
- "How does this relate to previous lessons?"

*Devotional:*
- "What is the key message for today?"
- "How can I apply this to my life?"

### Understanding Responses

- Answers include **citations** from sources
- Citations appear as numbered tags [1], [2], [3]
- Tap a citation to see the source
- Sources may be:
  - Devotionals
  - Quarterly lessons
  - Bible verses

### Best Practices

✅ **Do:**
- Ask specific questions
- Reference today's lesson/devotional
- Ask for clarification
- Request applications to daily life

❌ **Don't:**
- Ask for personal advice (e.g., medical, legal)
- Expect prophetic predictions
- Ask about unrelated topics

## Offline Mode

### Downloading Content

**For Offline Access:**

1. **Devotionals**: Automatically cached for last 7 days
2. **Quarterlies**: Tap "Download Quarterly" button
3. **Sermons**: Tap download icon on sermon detail

**Storage:**
- Devotionals: ~1MB per week
- Quarterlies: ~10MB per quarter
- Sermon audio: ~50-100MB each
- Sermon video: 200-500MB each

### Managing Downloads

1. Go to Settings → Storage
2. View downloaded content
3. Delete individual items or clear all

## Troubleshooting

### Common Issues

**App won't load:**
- Check internet connection
- Restart app
- Clear cache (Settings → Storage → Clear Cache)

**Video won't play:**
- Check internet speed (need 5+ Mbps for HD)
- Try lower quality setting
- Update app from Play Store

**Audio out of sync:**
- Close and reopen video
- Report issue if persistent

**Devotional not updating:**
- Check timezone in settings
- Pull down to refresh
- Verify internet connection

**AI Chat not responding:**
- Check internet connection
- Try shorter, simpler questions
- Restart app

**Downloads failing:**
- Check available storage
- Verify internet connection
- Try WiFi instead of mobile data

### Getting Help

**In-App Support:**

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: Is the app free?**
A: Yes! Completely free with no ads.

**Q: Do I need an account?**
A: No account required to use the app. Accounts are optional for syncing across devices.

**Q: Can I download sermons?**
A: Yes, but be mindful of storage space. Videos can be large.

**Q: How often are devotionals updated?**
A: Daily, every midnight in your timezone.

**Q: Can I suggest new features?**
A: Absolutely! Use the feedback form in Settings.

**Q: Is my chat history saved?**
A: No, chat sessions are temporary and not stored on servers.

**Q: Can I use this offline?**
A: Yes, download content first. Some features require internet.
